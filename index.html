About Dwight Partner:

Im a licensed family nurse practitioner in AZ, CA, and Nevada, I've worked as a nurse practitioner since 2008, and a registered nurse since 1979, and as a certified Health Teacher since 2006.

Why Choose Dwight?

Caring Provider

As a Nurse practitioner, I strive to create a warm and welcoming environment for my patients, and to listen to their concerns and needs with empathy and respect. I believe that caring for others is at the heart of my profession, and I take great pride in being able to make a positive difference in the lives of my patients.

Available 6 Days A Week

I am available for appointments six days a week during the evening time after 5pm. Whether it's a routine check-up or an unexpected health issue, I am here to provide the best possible care and support.

Free Follow Up Call

I understand that the road to recovery can sometimes have its ups and downs. That's why I am offering a free follow-up call to my patients to address any questions or concerns they may have about their treatment or progress.
